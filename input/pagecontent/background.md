### Background

The Belgian Cancer Registry (BCR / *Stichting Kankerregister*) collects, processes, and analyses cancer-related data from healthcare providers across Belgium.

This IG aims to standardise the exchange of:

- Cancer case identification and demographics
- Tumour characteristics (topography, morphology, behaviour, grade)
- Diagnosis, staging, and treatment events
- Follow-up and outcome data

Future revisions will align with the eHealth Belgium FHIR programme and existing European registry guides where applicable.
